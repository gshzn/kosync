Install KoSync client

Please follow the following steps to install the KoSync client on your Kobo device:
  1. Download the KoboRoot.tgz file here
  2. Move the KoboRoot.tgz into the hidden .kobo/ folder on your Kobo device.
  3. Reboot your Kobo device.
  4. See the KoSync menu button appear in the bottom right and click the Synchronise button.
